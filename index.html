WHO IS KATO?

Kato will hand you a lifestyle you can be proud of forever.

Kalen Imani Norton. Born and Raised in Long Beach, Ca on October 29,1981. 35 years of age.

As a former professional baseball player I have always had a passion for athletics. A 3 year NASM certified personal trainer and life long fitness enthusiast. Kato attacks fitness in an open minded, competitive, and enthusiastic fashion. Assessing the objectives of the client and implementing specific workout regiments. Constructing day to day interactive programs that translate into results. Kato has a no quick fix mentality. No limitless pills here. Just hard work that pays off. Dividends.

Kato will hand you a lifestyle you can be proud of forever. Kato made fitness a career when he realized how much he enjoyed seeing people's lives change in a positive way just from being fit. Through boosts in confidence, energy levels, lifestyle choices, etc...

Kato says, "Fitness is addicting just like anything else. Health encourages even healthier choices". Kalen noticed about 4 years ago that he wasn't a young pup anymore and if he wanted to stay fit he had to go about things differently. No more eating what I want. It was time to go all in. Diet, Weight Training, Flexibility, and so on. HEALTH being the number 1 priority.

Weather Kato is 47 or 60 years old, he'd like to be able to enjoy a nice game of catch!

Work Hard. Work Smart. Be Patient.

"The End"
